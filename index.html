DARK TOWER (PDC100) HP LOSS CALCULATOR

BY IDRIS



HP loss calculation is performed in 2 steps. First, the number of turns needed to defeat the enemy is computed by first calculating your damage:


Your damage = -(Enemy Defense - Player Attack)


The enemy HP is then divided by your damage and rounded up. If the number is negative, "???" is displayed. Next, the enemy's damage to you is calculated:


Enemy damage = -(Player Defense - Enemy Attack)


If enemy damage is negative, the HP loss is always 0. The final calculated HP Loss equals the number of turns multiplied by the enemy damage.
